# List of organisation

Listing of organisations involved in studies published on FReSH as an affiliation, sponsor or funder.  

Where information is available, each organisation is described by its standardised name, [SIREN](https://annuaire-entreprises.data.gouv.fr/) number, [ROR](https://ror.org/) number and/or website.  

This repository may be freely reused provided the citation rules are followed.
